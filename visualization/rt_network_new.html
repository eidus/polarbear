ABOUT

This network represents a random 1% sample of tweets collected during the 2010 Congressional midterm elections.


Nodes represent twitter users. Red nodes represent Republicans while blue nodes represent Democrats. Edges represent some form of interaction (RT or @mention) between the 2 users.

This visualization was presented by Josh Valdez and Daniel Ong, as part of Jeffrey Heer's Data Visualization course at Stanford
